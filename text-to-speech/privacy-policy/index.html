AI Multi Speaker TTS

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

Welcome to AI Multi Speaker TTS ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to helping you synthesize high-quality, professional, multi-speaker voiceovers and podcast scripts. We recognize that your text scripts, custom voices, and final audios are personal. Your data privacy and security are our top priorities.

Data Ownership Commitment

AI Multi Speaker TTS is designed to synthesize neural voice files from the text you type. We do not record your biological voice, we do not require microphone permissions, and we never use your custom typed scripts or rendered audio files for any purpose other than delivering them directly to you.

1. Information We Collect

To provide, save, and compile your multi-speaker projects, we collect a limited set of essential data:

  • Account Credentials: We use secure Firebase Authentication to log you in (including Anonymous sign-ins). This associates your scripts and audio projects to your unique User ID so you don't lose your work.
  • Script Metadata & Dialogue Blocks: Any text blocks, speaker names, and neural voice settings you enter are stored securely in Cloud Firestore. This lets you close the editor and resume editing your scripts later.
  • Synthesized Audio Outputs: When you generate a podcast, our secure VPS backend compiles your segments into a master MP3 file. This file is stored on Cloudflare R2 storage so you can stream, download, or share it.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We track anonymous performance metrics (like error logs and rendering times) to fix bugs and improve the latency of our speech synthesis pipeline.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data strictly to facilitate the audio generation and script storage workflow:

  • To synchronize your script drafts across your active sessions.
  • To securely stream the input text to our neural text-to-speech engine and return the audio bytes.
  • To host, stream, and deliver the final stitched MP3 file to your device.
  • To prevent abuse, resource exhaustion, or security threats on our servers.

3. Security & Storage Architecture

We deploy standard security protocols to safeguard your assets:

  • Private VPS Backend: Your text scripts are processed on our private, firewalled virtual private server.
  • Secure Database (Firestore): Project metadata is securely stored in Google Cloud Firestore and can only be accessed using your verified Firebase credentials.
  • Secure Distribution (Cloudflare R2): Your rendered MP3 files are stored on secure, S3-compatible Cloudflare R2 storage buckets and served over secure HTTPS links.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We integrate with a few trusted industry-standard infrastructure providers to process and deliver your services:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication and Firestore database hosting.
  • Cloudflare: For hosting your compiled MP3 audio files and routing network traffic securely.
  • Microsoft Azure / Edge TTS: To process and translate text strings into neural voice streams. No personal identifiers (like your user ID or email) are sent to the speech synthesis API.

5. Data Control & Deletion

You have full ownership of your data. You can delete individual text segments, or delete entire podcast projects directly from your Home Screen. Tapping "Delete" instantly and permanently removes the script blocks from our Firestore database. To request a complete deletion of your Firebase user account and any associated audio files on Cloudflare R2, you can contact us directly.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not targeted toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
