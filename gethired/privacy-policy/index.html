GetHired AI Resume & Cover Letter Maker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Welcome to GetHired, our AI Resume & Cover Letter Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). GetHired helps you build, tailor, and export polished resumes and cover letters with the help of AI. This policy explains what data the app collects, how we use it, and the controls you have over it.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver resume building, AI tailoring, and document export, we process the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up with email and password, sign in with a supported provider, or continue as a guest, Firebase Authentication issues a unique user ID we use to associate your data. We never see or store your password in plain text.
  • Resume & Cover Letter Content: Information you enter or upload, including your name, email, phone, location, links (e.g. GitHub, LinkedIn), professional summary, skills, education, work experience, projects, certifications, publications, and any custom sections or cover letters you create.
  • Uploaded Files: If you choose to import an existing CV (PDF or DOCX), the file is sent to our parsing service so we can extract structured data. We do not retain the original file after parsing completes.
  • Generated Output: The PDFs, HTML previews, or PNG previews we render at your request. Previews are generated on demand and not retained on our servers after the request completes.
  • Device Information: Standard, anonymous diagnostic data such as device model and operating system version, used to ensure compatibility and to debug crashes.
AI Processing Disclosure

GetHired uses AI to parse uploaded CVs, polish bullet points, generate tailored cover letters, and suggest improvements. When you trigger one of these features, the relevant text from your resume (and, for cover letters, the job description you provide) is sent to our secure backend to produce the result.

We do not use your resume or cover letter content to train AI models, and we do not sell or share this content with advertisers.

2. How We Store Your Data

  • On Your Device: Your active resume and cover letter drafts are stored locally using AsyncStorage so the app works smoothly even when offline. Generated PDFs are written to a temporary cache only long enough to share or save them, then removed.
  • Cloud Sync (Firebase Firestore): When you are signed in, your resumes and cover letters are securely synced to Google Firebase Firestore under your user ID so you can access them on a new device.
  • On Our Servers: Files you upload for parsing and the data we generate (PDF, HTML, PNG) are processed in temporary, isolated job folders that are deleted automatically after each request completes. We do not maintain a long-term server-side copy of your resume.

3. Permissions We Request

GetHired only requests permissions that are needed for a feature you actively use:

  • Files / Documents: When you import a CV, your device's document picker is used so you can choose the file. We only receive the single file you select.
  • Storage Access (Android): When you tap "Download" to save a generated PDF to your phone, Android's Storage Access Framework asks you to choose a folder. The PDF is written only to the folder you pick — no broad storage permission is granted.
  • Sharing: When you tap "Share," we hand the PDF off to your operating system's share sheet so you can send it via email, messaging, cloud storage, or any other app you choose.

GetHired does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted partners to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase Authentication: For sign-up, sign-in, and anonymous "guest" sessions.
  • Google Firebase Firestore: For securely syncing your resumes and cover letters to your account.
  • Oaknova Systems API: Our backend service, which performs AI-assisted resume parsing, bullet polishing, cover letter generation, and PDF/HTML/PNG rendering.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For distributing the app and processing any future in-app purchases. We never see or store your payment information.

5. Data Retention & Your Controls

You remain in control of your data at all times:

  • Edit or Delete Content: You can edit or delete any resume or cover letter directly inside the app.
  • Local Reset: Signing out or clearing the app's storage removes all local resume data from your device.
  • Account & Cloud Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and all associated cloud data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Once your account is deleted, your resumes and cover letters in Firestore are permanently removed.
  • Temporary Files: Files generated during preview, share, or download are removed from your device's cache after use.

6. Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Firebase Authentication handles password hashing and token-based session security on our behalf. Access to your synced data in Firestore is restricted by per-user security rules, so only the signed-in account that owns a resume can read or write it.

7. Children's Privacy

GetHired is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete the data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Material changes will also be communicated within the app where appropriate. Your continued use of GetHired after an update means you accept the revised policy.
