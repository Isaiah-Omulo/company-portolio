AI Interview Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Welcome to AI Interview Prep & Mock ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide an advanced AI-driven platform to help job seekers prepare for interviews through realistic simulations and expert feedback. Your privacy and data security are our highest priorities.

1. Information We Collect

To provide personalized mock interviews and career coaching, we collect the following information:

  • Microphone Access (Audio): We require access to your device's microphone to capture your verbal responses during mock interviews. This is essential for the "Audio-In" simulation features.
  • CV & Resume Data: When you upload a PDF/DOCX or paste your resume text, we extract professional information (skills, experience, education) to tailor the interview questions to your background.
  • Job Descriptions: We process the job descriptions you provide to ensure the AI interviewer asks relevant, role-specific questions.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app (e.g., number of sessions, practice streaks) to improve our coaching algorithms.
AI Processing & Third-Party Data Sharing

AI Interview Prep & Mock relies on third-party Artificial Intelligence services to deliver its core features. The first time you open the app you are shown a dedicated AI Data Sharing Disclosure screen that lists exactly what data is sent, who it is sent to, and asks for your explicit permission. You cannot continue using the app's AI features without giving that permission. The same information is summarised below.

What we send, to whom, and what happens to it:

  • Voice recordings → Deepgram (United States): Audio captured by your microphone while you answer is sent to Deepgram for speech-to-text transcription only. Audio is processed in transit and is automatically deleted from our processing servers as soon as the transcription completes. It is never used for voice or biometric identification, and per Deepgram's API terms it is not used to train any AI model.
  • CV / resume text, job descriptions, your answers and reflections → Groq (United States): The text we extract from your CV, the job descriptions you paste, and your typed or transcribed answers are sent to Groq's large language models so they can generate questions, scores, and coaching feedback for you. Per Groq's API terms, this text is processed only to fulfil your request and is not used to train their models.
  • All of the above → Oaknova Systems (our backend): Acts as the secure relay between your device and the AI providers above. Stores your session history (parsed CV, transcripts, scores, reflections) against an anonymous random account ID — never your real name, email, or phone number — so you can review your progress across devices. We do not share your data with anyone outside the providers listed in this section, and we never sell it.

You stay in control. Your voice recordings are deleted automatically after transcription. Everything else is stored against your anonymous account so that you can review your sessions, and you can permanently delete any single session or your entire account from inside the app at any time (Settings → Delete account/data). Deletion is final and propagates to our backend databases.

Equal protection commitment. Each third-party AI provider listed above is bound by contractual data-protection terms (Data Processing Agreements) that require them to provide the same or equivalent level of protection for your data as we do, prohibit using your data to train their models, and prohibit any biometric identification of your voice.

2. How We Store Your Data

We use a hybrid storage model designed so that you can access your history across devices while remaining anonymous to us:

  • Anonymous account: Your account is identified only by a random Firebase ID generated on your device. We do not ask for, collect, or store your real name, email, or phone number. Your CV, sessions, and feedback are linked only to this anonymous ID.
  • Cloud persistence (text only): Your parsed Resume JSON, job descriptions, transcripts, and AI feedback are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure (PostgreSQL and Firebase Firestore) against your anonymous ID so you can review your progress on any device you sign in from. This data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest by the underlying cloud providers.
  • Audio handling (auto-delete): Voice recordings sent for transcription are temporary. As soon as Speech-to-Text completes, the audio file is automatically deleted from our processing servers. We do not keep audio archives.
  • Your deletion controls: You can delete any uploaded resume or past interview session from inside the app at any time, and the deletion is propagated to our databases. You can also delete your entire account from the Settings tab, which permanently wipes all data associated with your anonymous ID.
  • No selling, no advertising: We do not sell your data, we do not run third-party advertising in the app, and we do not share your data with anyone outside the AI providers explicitly named in this policy.

4. Your Consent & Control Over AI Data Sharing

Because the app's core features depend on third-party AI processing, we ask for your explicit, informed consent before any data is shared:

  • Dedicated in-app disclosure: On first launch (and again whenever this disclosure materially changes), the app shows a separate AI Data Sharing screen listing what data is sent and to whom. You must tap "I Agree & Continue" before any data leaves your device.
  • Microphone permission: A separate operating-system prompt asks for microphone access the first time you record an answer. You can revoke microphone access at any time from your device settings.
  • Withdrawing consent: You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data from within the app (Settings → Delete account/data) and by uninstalling the app. You can also email us at contact@oaknovasystems.com to request deletion.

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your professional data:

  • Deletion: You can delete any uploaded resume or past interview session from within the app at any time.
  • Account Termination: If you wish to delete your entire profile and all associated data, you may do so via the Settings tab, which will trigger a permanent deletion from our databases.
