AI Interview Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Welcome to AI Interview Prep & Mock ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide an advanced AI-driven platform to help job seekers prepare for interviews through realistic simulations and expert feedback. Your privacy and data security are our highest priorities.

1. Information We Collect

To provide personalized mock interviews and career coaching, we collect the following information:

  • Microphone Access (Audio): We require access to your device's microphone to capture your verbal responses during mock interviews. This is essential for the "Audio-In" simulation features.
  • CV & Resume Data: When you upload a PDF/DOCX or paste your resume text, we extract professional information (skills, experience, education) to tailor the interview questions to your background.
  • Job Descriptions: We process the job descriptions you provide to ensure the AI interviewer asks relevant, role-specific questions.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app (e.g., number of sessions, practice streaks) to improve our coaching algorithms.
AI Processing & Third-Party Data Sharing

AI Interview Prep & Mock relies on third-party Artificial Intelligence services to deliver its core features. The first time you open the app you are shown a dedicated AI Data Sharing Disclosure screen that lists exactly what data is sent, who it is sent to, and asks for your explicit permission. You cannot continue using the app's AI features without giving that permission. The same information is summarised below.

What we send and to whom:

  • Voice recordings → Deepgram (United States): Audio captured by your microphone while you answer a practice or mock interview question is sent to Deepgram for speech-to-text transcription. Audio is processed in transit and deleted from processing servers once transcription completes.
  • CV / resume text, job descriptions, transcribed answers → Groq (United States): The text we extract from your CV, the job descriptions you paste, and the text of your answers are sent to Groq's large language models to generate interview questions, scores, and coaching feedback.
  • All of the above → Oaknova Systems (our backend): Acts as the relay between your device and the AI providers above, stores your session history linked to your anonymous Firebase ID, and returns AI feedback to your device.

Equal protection commitment: Each third-party AI provider listed above is bound by contractual data-protection terms (Data Processing Agreements) that require them to provide the same or equivalent level of protection for your data as we do. Your voice data is used solely for the duration of the interview, is not used for biometric identification, and is not used to train any AI model.

2. How We Store Your Data

We use a hybrid storage model to ensure data availability across your devices:

  • Cloud Persistence: Your parsed Resume JSON and Interview Transcripts are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure (PostgreSQL and Firebase Firestore) linked to your anonymous Firebase UID. This allows you to view your progress and history.
  • Audio Handling: Audio recordings sent for processing are treated as temporary. Once the transcription (Speech-to-Text) is completed, the temporary audio files are deleted from our processing servers.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

Premium features (Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers) are processed through Apple App Store and Google Play Store using RevenueCat.

  • We do not collect or store your credit card or financial details.
  • We receive only the confirmation of your subscription status to unlock premium AI features.

4. Your Consent & Control Over AI Data Sharing

Because the app's core features depend on third-party AI processing, we ask for your explicit, informed consent before any data is shared:

  • Dedicated in-app disclosure: On first launch (and again whenever this disclosure materially changes), the app shows a separate AI Data Sharing screen listing what data is sent and to whom. You must tap "I Agree & Continue" before any data leaves your device.
  • Microphone permission: A separate operating-system prompt asks for microphone access the first time you record an answer. You can revoke microphone access at any time from your device settings.
  • Withdrawing consent: You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your data from within the app (Settings → Delete account/data) and by uninstalling the app. You can also email us at contact@oaknovasystems.com to request deletion.

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your professional data:

  • Deletion: You can delete any uploaded resume or past interview session from within the app at any time.
  • Account Termination: If you wish to delete your entire profile and all associated data, you may do so via the Settings tab, which will trigger a permanent deletion from our databases.
