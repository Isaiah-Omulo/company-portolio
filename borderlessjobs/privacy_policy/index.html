Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Welcome to Borderless Jobs: Remote Work ("we," "our," or "us"). We are dedicated to helping you find the best remote career opportunities worldwide while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy. Your trust is our priority.

Local Data Commitment

Borderless Jobs is designed to keep your personal browsing and job-saving activity private. Most of the data you generate within the app—such as your saved jobs and search preferences—is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

While we prioritize local storage, we collect a limited amount of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Saved Jobs & Preferences: Any jobs you save or preferences you set are stored locally on your device's internal storage. We do not have access to this data.
  • Push Notifications: If you opt-in to notifications, we collect a unique device token (Push Token) to send you alerts about new jobs or app updates.
  • Usage Data: We use anonymous analytics tools (like Firebase and Mixpanel) to collect technical data such as device type, operating system, and app interactions to help us squash bugs and improve the user experience.
  • Subscription Data: For premium features, we use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. This involves anonymous identifiers to track your purchase status across devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To deliver remote job alerts and notifications.
  • To manage and verify your premium subscription status.
  • To analyze app performance and usage trends to improve our features.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

All premium transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to facilitate these transactions.

  • We do not see, collect, or store your credit card or billing information.
  • Subscription management is handled directly through your respective App Store account settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with a few trusted service providers to enhance our app's functionality:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and purchase validation.
  • Firebase/Mixpanel: For anonymous analytics and app performance monitoring.
  • Expo: To facilitate push notification delivery.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your saved jobs and preferences are stored locally, you have total control over them. Uninstalling the Borderless Jobs app will permanently delete all locally stored data from your device. For data held by third parties (like notification tokens or anonymous analytics), you may contact us to request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
