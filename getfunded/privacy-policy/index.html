Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2024

Welcome to GetFunded: Compare Prop Firms ("we," "our," or "us"). GetFunded is a financial data comparison tool built for the trading community. Our goal is to provide transparency in the prop firm industry while protecting user privacy.

Affiliate Disclosure

GetFunded provides independent comparison data. To keep this service free, we may receive compensation when you click on links and purchase challenges from prop firms listed in our app. This does not affect the cost to you or our data integrity.

1. Information We Collect

GetFunded is designed to be used without an account. We collect minimal information to provide the service:

  • Regional Data: We may detect your general location (Country level) via IP address to hide prop firms that are restricted in your region.
  • Device Information: Standard anonymized data such as device type and OS version for crash reporting and app performance.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized tracking of which firms or categories are most popular to improve our comparison engine.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Links

Our app contains links to third-party websites (Prop Firms). When you click an affiliate link:

  • You are redirected to the firm's website.
  • A "cookie" or tracking identifier may be placed by the firm's website to identify GetFunded as the referring partner.
  • Please review the privacy policies of those individual firms as we do not control their data collection practices.

3. No Financial Advice

GetFunded provides data for educational and informational purposes only. We do not provide financial, investment, or trading advice. Trading Forex, Futures, and Crypto involves significant risk. Always perform your own due diligence before purchasing a trading challenge.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to facilitate the app:

  • Google Firebase: For hosting our comparison database and app infrastructure.
  • Expo: To facilitate the application framework and performance monitoring.

5. Data Retention

Because we do not require user accounts, we do not store personal profiles. Any anonymized performance data is kept only as long as necessary to improve the app's functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

GetFunded is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18, as trading is an adult activity.
