Product Video Generator

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

Welcome to Product Video Generator ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to helping you create stunning marketing videos for your products while ensuring the complete privacy and security of your visual assets.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our video generation services, we require access to the following:

  • Photo Library Access: We access your gallery solely to allow you to select product screenshots, images, and logos for your video. We only process the specific images you choose to upload.
  • Image Data: We process the pixels of your uploaded photos to analyze product features, extract relevant content, and generate animated video sequences.
  • Device Information: We may collect standard anonymous data such as device type and operating system version to ensure app compatibility and security.
AI Processing & Data Deletion

Product Video Generator utilizes Google Gemini AI to analyze your product images and screenshots to produce high-quality ad videos. Your images are sent to our secure processing infrastructure solely for analysis and video generation. Once the video is produced, all uploaded images and screenshots are immediately and permanently deleted from our systems. We do not use your photos for AI model training or permanent archiving.

2. How We Store Your Data

We prioritize your privacy by minimizing data retention:

  • Temporary Processing: Your images are held in volatile memory only for the duration of the analysis and video generation cycle. As stated above, they are deleted immediately after use.
  • Local Storage: Your generated videos and application history are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a cloud gallery of your videos.
  • Secured Access: We use industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect your data during the brief window it is being processed.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

We offer premium features through subscription plans. All payments are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store (via RevenueCat).

  • We do not see or store your credit card or bank information.
  • Payment confirmation is handled via standard store-provided validation services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted partners to provide our service:

  • Google Gemini: For AI-powered image analysis.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For secure, high-speed video rendering and processing.
  • App Stores: For app distribution and secure payment processing.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. Since your history and videos are stored locally, deleting them within the app or uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device. Because we do not store your images on our servers after processing, there is no server-side image data to delete.
