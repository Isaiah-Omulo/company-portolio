Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

Welcome to WhoseTurn: Shared Bills & Tasks ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a transparent way to manage group responsibilities.

Camera Usage Disclosure

WhoseTurn requests access to your Camera and Photo Gallery. This is used exclusively to allow you to upload photos of receipts or proof of task completion. Using the camera is entirely optional and photos are only shared with members of your specific group.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you sign up, we collect your name and email address to manage your account and group memberships via Google Firebase.
  • Group Data: We store names of groups, task titles, and rotation orders that you create to facilitate the app’s core functionality.
  • Content: If you choose to upload an "Evidence Photo," that image is stored securely in our database to be viewed by your group members.
  • Device Information: We collect anonymized device identifiers to send push notifications (nudges) for upcoming turns.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Maintain the fair rotation of tasks within your groups.
  • Send reminders and notifications regarding due bills.
  • Provide a history of "who did what" to ensure transparency.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is only shared in the following contexts:

  • With Group Members: Your name and any proof of completion photos you upload are visible to other members of the groups you join.
  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase (Database/Auth) and Expo (Framework) to host and operate the app.

4. Your Controls

You can manage your data at any time:

  • Permissions: You can revoke Camera or Notification access via your device settings at any time.
  • Deletion: You may leave groups or delete your account, which will remove your personal identifiers from our active rotation records.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only members of a group can see that group’s tasks and photos.
