Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2024

Welcome to ApplyAI: Job Tracker & Resume ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to helping you organize your job hunt through intelligent automation while keeping your data secure and private.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our job tracking and AI analysis services, we process the following types of information:

  • Resume Data: When you upload a resume (PDF, DOCX) or paste resume text, we extract professional information such as skills, work history, and education.
  • Job Application Details: Information you manually enter about jobs, including Company Name, Position, Job URLs, Application Status (e.g., "Interviewing"), and your personal notes.
  • Device Information: Standard anonymous data including device type and operating system to ensure app compatibility.
AI Processing Disclosure

ApplyAI uses Google Gemini AI to analyze your resume and job descriptions. This allows us to generate match scores, cover letters, and interview questions. Your resume data is sent to Google's API solely for generation purposes and is handled according to enterprise privacy standards.

2. How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: Your job application list and status preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage).
  • Cloud Database: Structured resume data is stored on our secure PostgreSQL servers to allow the AI features to function across different job analyzes without re-uploading your file every time.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

We offer "Pro" subscriptions to unlock unlimited AI tools. All payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

  • We do not see or store your credit card or bank information.
  • We use RevenueCat to manage and validate your subscription status via an anonymous App User ID.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted partners to provide our service:

  • Google Cloud/Gemini: For AI text analysis and resume parsing.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management.
  • PostgreSQL/Hosted Infrastructure: For secure data persistence.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual job applications within the app at any time. If you wish to have your stored resume data removed from our AI database, you may use the "Change Resume" feature or contact us directly.
