Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

Welcome to IvyCrypto: Crypto Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are dedicated to providing you with the most accurate market data and portfolio tools while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy.

Price Alert Disclosure

IvyCrypto requests permission to send Push Notifications. This is critical for the "Price Alarms" feature, allowing us to notify you instantly when a cryptocurrency hits your target price. You can manage these alerts and permissions at any time within your device settings.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your email address and an encrypted password to secure your personal watchlists and portfolio data.
  • Portfolio & Watchlist: We store the coin identifiers, purchase prices, and amounts you manually enter to calculate your profit/loss and display your personalized market view.
  • Notification Tokens: To deliver real-time price alerts, we store a unique, anonymized Push Token associated with your device.
  • Device Data: We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) to ensure compatibility and optimize the performance of our market widgets.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Sync your crypto portfolio across multiple devices.
  • Monitor market movements and trigger your custom price alarms.
  • Provide accurate 24h gain/loss analytics based on your holdings.
  • Process premium subscriptions and manage "Ad-Free" experiences.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal financial data. However, we use the following trusted providers to operate IvyCrypto:

  • CoinGecko: To fetch market metadata, logos, and historical price charts.
  • RevenueCat: To manage Apple App Store and Google Play Store subscriptions securely.
  • Google AdMob: To serve advertisements to our free-tier users. AdMob may collect anonymized IDs for ad personalization.
  • PostgreSQL & Redis: Used to host our secure database and real-time price cache.

4. Your Controls

You maintain full control over your information:

  • Data Export/View: You can view all your stored portfolio data directly in the app's "Portfolio" tab.
  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account at any time within the Profile settings, which will permanently remove your email, watchlist, and alarms from our database.
  • Ad Preferences: Free users can manage ad personalization through their Google or Apple account settings.

5. Security

IvyCrypto uses industry-standard SSL encryption and JWT (JSON Web Tokens) to ensure that your portfolio and account data are accessible only by you. We use salted password hashing to ensure your credentials remain protected.
