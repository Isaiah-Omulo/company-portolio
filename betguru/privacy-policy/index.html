Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Welcome to Betguru ("we," "our," or "us"). Betguru is a football analysis and prediction application dedicated to providing statistical data and tips. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We believe in privacy by design. To use Betguru, you are not required to create an account, provide your name, email address, or phone number.

We do not store personally identifiable information (PII) on our servers.

2. Information We Automatically Collect

While we do not collect personal profiles, we use third-party services to ensure the app functions correctly. These services may collect:

  • Device Information: Anonymous data such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (UUID) to manage app preferences.
  • Usage Data: Aggregated, anonymous data regarding which screens are viewed or which buttons are clicked, used solely to improve the app experience.
  • Subscription Status: We track the validity of your "VIP" subscriptions (Platinum, Silver, Gold) via an anonymous App User ID.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

We offer paid access to premium content ("Guru Elite", "Platinum", "Silver", "Gold").

  • Payment Processing: All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see, process, or store your credit card or bank details.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to validate your purchase receipt. RevenueCat associates your purchase with an anonymous user ID to grant you access to premium content.
4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party providers:

  • Google Firebase: Used for hosting our database of match predictions and authentication tokens (anonymous).
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscription infrastructure.

5. Responsible Gambling & Age Restriction

Strictly 18+: Betguru is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from or market to individuals under the age of 18.

Disclaimer: The content provided in Betguru is for informational and entertainment purposes only. We are not a gambling operator. We do not accept money for bets. Past performance of our "Super Computer" or "VIP Tips" is not a guarantee of future results. Please bet responsibly.

6. Data Security

Although we do not hold personal data, we implement security measures to protect the integrity of our app and the anonymous data we process via Firebase and RevenueCat.
