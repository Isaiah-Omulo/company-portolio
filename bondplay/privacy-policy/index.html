Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 08, 2024

Welcome to BondPlay ("we," "our," or "us"). BondPlay is designed to help couples strengthen their relationship through shared interactive experiences. Because your relationship is private, we treat your data with the highest level of care.

Our Commitment to Privacy

BondPlay operates on a Peer-to-Peer Data Model. This means your game answers, chat messages, and moods are shared only with your linked partner. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or advertisers.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a synchronized experience between you and your partner, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile photo provided via Authentication.
  • Relationship Linking: We store a unique coupleId to securely connect your account with your partner's account.
  • Game Data: Your responses to game decks, ranking preferences, and saved "Memories" generated during gameplay.
  • Mood Logs: Historical data of your daily mood selections to help you and your partner track emotional trends.
  • Messaging: Text content and system-generated messages sent within the shared couple's chat.

2. Hardware Permissions

BondPlay requires the following permissions to provide the full experience:

  • Notifications: To alert you when your partner invites you to a game, updates their mood, or sends a message.
  • Camera/Gallery: To allow you to upload a profile picture.

3. Data Storage and Security

We utilize Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage) for data management. All data is protected by industry-standard encryption and Firebase Security Rules, ensuring that your private "Couple Space" can only be accessed by you and your verified partner.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted service providers to run BondPlay:

  • Firebase: For core infrastructure, database, and user authentication.
  • RevenueCat: To manage premium subscriptions and digital purchases securely.
  • Expo: To facilitate the application framework and crash reporting.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We believe you should have total control over your relationship data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete individual memories or mood logs directly within the app.
  • Account Deletion: If you choose to delete your account, all personal data associated with your user ID will be permanently removed from our active databases.
  • Couple Unlinking: If a relationship is unlinked, the shared coupleId data remains accessible only if both parties agree, otherwise it can be requested for deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

BondPlay is intended for use by individuals aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.
