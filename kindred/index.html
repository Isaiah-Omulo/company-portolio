Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Welcome to Kindred ("we," "our," or "us"), a peer support platform provided by Oaknova Systems LTD. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe space to share your experiences.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Service:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name.
  • User Generated Content (UGC): We store the stories, replies, and mood data you voluntarily post to the platform.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data on how you interact with the app (e.g., circles joined, buttons clicked) to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To facilitate peer support interactions within Circles.
  • To enforce our community guidelines and ensure user safety.
  • To notify you about activity on your posts (via Push Notifications).
3. User Generated Content & Safety (Zero Tolerance Policy)

Kindred is a safe space. We have a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive users.

  • Content Moderation: All stories and replies are subject to review. We reserve the right to remove any content that promotes hate speech, harassment, violence, or self-harm without prior notice.
  • Reporting: Users can report inappropriate content or abusive users directly within the app via the "Report" function found on every story and reply. Reports are reviewed by our team within 24 hours.
  • Blocking: Users have the ability to block other users. Once blocked, you will no longer see content from that user.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

Deleting Your Account: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the app by navigating to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This action is permanent and erases your stories, replies, and profile information from our servers immediately.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to help us operate:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication, database hosting, and analytics.
  • RevenueCat: For processing "Kindred+" subscription payments. We do not store your credit card information directly.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@connectkissmigrate.com
  • By visiting our website: www.connectkissmigrate.com
