AI Passport

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

Welcome to AI Passport: ID & Passport Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to providing a highly transactional, fast-track utility for creating compliant document photos while ensuring your data remains private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our specialized photo creation services, we require access to the following:

  • Camera Access: We use your device's camera to capture real-time portrait photos. This is necessary for the face alignment and background removal features to function.
  • Photo Library Access: If you choose to upload an existing photo, we access your gallery solely to process that specific image.
  • Image Data: We process the pixels of your uploaded or captured photos to detect faces, remove backgrounds, and adjust lighting to meet official document standards.
  • Device Information: Standard anonymous data including device type and operating system to ensure app compatibility and security.
AI Processing Disclosure

AI Passport uses advanced Artificial Intelligence to automate background removal, face centering, and compliance verification. Your image data is sent to our secure processing infrastructure solely for generation purposes. We do not use your photos for AI model training or identification purposes.

2. How We Store Your Data

As a privacy-focused utility, we prioritize local storage over cloud persistence:

  • Local-Only History: Your generated photos and application history are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage) and secure local file directories. We do not maintain a cloud gallery of your photos.
  • Temporary Processing: Photos sent to our processing infrastructure are held temporarily in volatile memory only for the duration of the processing cycle and are not permanently archived on our servers.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

We offer premium features to unlock high-resolution, watermark-free document sheets. All payments are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

  • We do not see or store your credit card or bank information.
  • Payment confirmation is handled via standard store-provided validation services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted partners to provide our service:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: For secure, high-speed AI image processing.
  • App Stores: For app distribution and secure payment processing.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. Since your history is stored locally, deleting a photo from the "My Photos" tab or uninstalling the app will permanently remove all associated data from your device. If you wish to request deletion of any temporary processing logs, please contact us.
