Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

Welcome to FairSpeak: Language Exchange ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and effective environment for language learners to connect and improve their skills using AI-driven tools.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate language learning and peer-to-peer exchange, we collect the following:

  • Account Data: Email address and profile information provided during authentication.
  • User-Generated Content: Text messages, images, and audio voice notes sent in "Lounge" rooms or Private Messages.
  • Pronunciation Data: Audio recordings specifically submitted to the "Pronunciation Studio" for AI scoring.
AI Processing Disclosure

FairSpeak utilizes Google Gemini AI to analyze text for grammar explanations, generate reply suggestions, and evaluate pronunciation. Audio recordings and text snippets are processed by Gemini APIs to provide feedback. This data is handled in accordance with enterprise-grade privacy standards and is not used to train public AI models.

2. Hardware Permissions

FairSpeak requires specific device permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): Required to allow users to take and send photos within the chat lounges and update profile pictures.
  • Microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): Required for the "Pronunciation Studio" and for sending voice notes to other users.
  • Storage: Required to save temporary audio recordings and upload existing images from your gallery.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase for our infrastructure:

  • Cloud Firestore: Stores chat history and user profile data securely.
  • Firebase Storage: Stores images and audio files. Access is restricted via strict security rules requiring authenticated user sessions.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

FairSpeak offers premium tiers (Gold, Silver, Platinum). All financial transactions are managed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription logic; we never see or store your credit card details.

5. Third-Party Services

We partner with the following providers:

  • Google Gemini: For language analysis and AI tutoring.
  • Firebase (Google): For database, storage, and authentication.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management.

6. Data Deletion

You may delete your messages or account at any time. To request a full deletion of your stored data from our servers, please contact us at the email provided below.
